About Markethon 4.0
Markethon is an annual 24-hour hackathon that brings together creative, out-of-the-box people
to solve real cases
from large companies.
Markethon 4.0 sections
Graphic and Motion design
Examples: logo design, brand identity design, product package design, animation design
Marketing strategy
Examples: increasing sales, advertising campaign, building a positive brand image
UI/UX Web design
Examples: website redesign, create application, interface development
